Tree Service FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions About Tree Service

How do I Choose The Right Tree Service Provider for My Needs?

Be sure that the tree service you hire has proper insurance and a valid license before you hire them. You don't want some uninsured amateur to drop a 1.5-ton tree on your home. Ensure they have an arborist on staff who is certified by the International Society of Arboriculture (ISA). That will guarantee that a qualified arborist provides trustworthy recommendations for caring for your trees.

How Much Does A Tree Removal Cost?

The average person has no idea how much it costs to cut a tree down, so the final cost often comes as a shock. Apple tree removal can be very different from oak tree removal. The price can vary depending on several factors, including:


  • Whether the tree is in the front or back yard
  • If there are any fences or power lines nearby
  • If there are underground wires
  • How close the house is to the tree

For the most accurate and precise estimate, it's best to have a professional arborist inspect the land.

Will The Tree Service Equipment Damage My Lawn?

No, assuming you went with a reputable tree service. We use the newest man lift technology, gentle on grass, and won't leave craters. We take great care to avoid destroying your pristine lawn by strategically placing where we drop trees and logs. To prevent ruts and divots in your yard, we will not hesitate to put our workforce to use by removing records from your property.

Who Removes The Tree And Firewood

It's up to the buyer to decide. Customers typically have us clear out the entire site, including any firewood, but occasionally one will ask to keep the wood. Leaving everything in place can be cost-effective.
